How Ranking Works

Everything you need to know about climbing the ladder

◈

Rank Rating (RR)

You earn or lose RR after every match. Reach 100 RR to rank up. Drop to 0 and lose enough to rank down.

◎

Placement Matches

At the start of each act you play 5 placement matches. Your results decide where you are placed on the ladder.

⚔

Act Resets

Each new act partially resets your rank. You keep a badge of your peak rank from the previous act.

◉

Performance Bonus

Playing well individually can boost your RR gain even in a loss, rewarding skill over pure win rate.
